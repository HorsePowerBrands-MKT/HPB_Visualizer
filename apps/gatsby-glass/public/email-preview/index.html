Gatsby Glass — Email Previews

Static renders of every transactional email the visualizer sends. These are generated directly from the production renderers in packages/api-handlers/src/resend.ts, so what you see here is exactly what Resend will deliver. Regenerate with pnpm preview:emails after editing the templates.

SAS · Save & Send to Me — sent to the customer

    Sent when the customer clicks "Save & Send to Me" after configuring an enclosure manually.
    Subject Your Gatsby Glass Design Preview
    To Customer (the email entered on the form)
  • SAS · Customer · Inspiration mode →
    Sent after the customer uploads an inspiration photo and clicks "Save & Send to Me".
    Subject Your Gatsby Glass Design Preview

RAQ · Request an Estimate — sent to the franchise

  • RAQ · Franchise · Inspiration mode →
